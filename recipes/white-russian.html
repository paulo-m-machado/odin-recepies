White Russian

The white russian was invented in the 60s as an iteration on the black russian, neither of these drinks is russian, but their names refer to the vodka present in the cocktails

It became a popular drink thanks to the 1998 movie "The Big Lebowski"

Ingredients

  • Vodka
  • Coffee liqueur
  • Heavy cream
  • ice (optional)

Steps

  1. Add 2 ounces of vodka and 1 ounce of coffee liqueur to a glass filled with ice
  2. Top with 1 splash of heavy cream and stir
